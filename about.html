About PolyStrike

Risk infrastructure for the prediction market era.

PolyStrike is an analytical toolkit that brings options-style risk management to prediction markets. We help traders make decisions based on risk analysis, not intuition. No trade execution. No custody. No financial advice. Just tools that teach risk management and reduce impulsive trading.

Core Principles

What we believe.

Prediction Markets = Option Chains

Every bracket is a strike. Every price is a premium. The payout is binary. Once you see it this way, traditional risk tools become directly applicable.

Risk Management > Alpha

Even the best thesis fails without position sizing. Tools that show risk before the trade save more money than any signal.

Public Good Infrastructure

Better-informed traders = better price discovery = healthier markets. Our tools improve the ecosystem for everyone.
