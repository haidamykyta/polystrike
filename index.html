Why hedge a prediction market bet?

Binary Risk

Polymarket pays $1 or $0. A bracket bet at $0.60 means risking $0.60 to make $0.40. Miss the bracket and the entire position is gone.

Time Decay

A position priced at $0.99 can drop to $0.15 in hours if BTC moves 5%. The path between open and close is where the real risk lives.

Bounded Loss

Add one Bybit vanilla option. Max drawdown drops from -100% to -25%. Time decay converts temporary drawdown into profit by expiry.

Key Insight

The red curve (now) shows a temporary drawdown of -12% for extreme moves. Time decay converts this into profit: by expiry, the entire range from $55K to $85K is profitable. Your worst case is -2% in the flat zone. That's what hedging does.
